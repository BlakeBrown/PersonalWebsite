Hello World

Welcome to my website! My name is Blake Brown and I am an engineering student, full stack web developer and aspiring entrepreneur. My goal in life is simple; I want to help advance society using my immense passion for science and technology. As a software developer I build exceptional web, mobile and desktop applications, but I am also highly interested in learning hardware and circuit design as I study electrical engineering at the University of Waterloo.

One of my strongest traits is that I have a tremendous amount of dedication for everything that I do. Within the past year I have placed in the top 10% of my class and achieved the highest possible assessment on two consecutive co-op terms. In terms of extracurriculars I also became an academic class representative, started an intramural soccer team and learned new tricks at the juggling club. I do not believe, however, that mere accomplishments define one’s success. To me, working consistently harder on becoming a better person is the most important objective one can have. We all have our own set of goals and achievements, but effort and strive is what determines where we are in a few years from now. That is why I place a huge emphasis on learning from my mistakes. As Mark Zuckerberg once said, it is crucial to “move fast and break things”.

In my free time, I have a huge passion for the plethora of exciting activities this world has to offer and I absolutely love wakeboarding, rollerblading and juggling. I also play piano, unicycle and solve Robik’s cubes – I have many hobbies so I’m sure we have some in common!

Thank you for reading my small excerpt, I hope you learned something new about me. Enjoy your stay at my website and till next time, bon voyage!
