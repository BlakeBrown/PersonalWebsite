Filespace

Filespace is the ultimate online storage system. In order to solve the issue of having to organize your files across several devices and operating systems, the application integrates several cloud storage services (Dropbox, Google Drive, etc) together in order to create one gigantic file storage system. As a hackathon project at HackWestern, I worked on setting up most of the backend. I integrated both the Dropbox and Google Drive API's together. I also used single-sign on (SSO) for the authentication process, so users can authenticate once and forget about having to log on the next time they want to download files onto their device.

Technologies used: Dropbox and Google Drive API's, Node.js, Express, HTML, CSS, Javascript
