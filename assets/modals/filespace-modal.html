Filespace

A team project created at HackWestern, Filespace is the ultimate online storage system. In order to solve the common issue of organizing your files across multiple devices and operating systems, my team came up with the innovative solution of combining several cloud storage services to provide huge amounts of free storage with easy access from almost anywhere. As the backend developer in this project, I worked on integrating Dropbox and Google Drive together to act as our unified storage system. I used single-sign on (SSO) for the authentication process, so users can authenticate once and forget about having to log on the next time they want to download files onto their device.
