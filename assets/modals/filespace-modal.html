An alternative to Netflix & Chill, Noflix is the perfect iOS app when you want to try something new. The application asks you to enter a simple price range and general category, and then uses a wide combination of API’s to find an exciting nearby attraction. Noflix will then call an Uber to come pick you up, but you don’t get to find out where you’re going till you get there - it's an adventure! You could end up anywhere, from concerts to shopping to carnivals or restaurants... it's a surprise!

Technologies used: Dropbox and Google Drive API's, Node.js, HTML, CSS, Javascript
